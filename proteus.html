Proteus


Proteus is an Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV). The focus of the project is to design and manufacture an AUV as autonomous and efficient as it can be.

Proteus shall be used in various activities to represent the university and compete in the International RoboSub Competition.

Proteus is our first autonomous underwater vehicle, is not only our first but the first in Puerto Rico and the Caribbean. Proteus is aimed to help students learn about autonomy and all the systems that go in to building an autonomous vehicle.

The Vehicle as been a year and a half in the making, it is our first time competing in the Robosub competition, and all of our members have learned useful skills for AUV development.

Proteus is going to be completed by july 2017, to successfully compete in the 20th robosub competition representing the University of Puerto Rico mayaguez campus.
