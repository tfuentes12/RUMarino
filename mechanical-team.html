Mechanical Design & Manufacturing

  • Design & Manufacturing


The mechanical team is in charge of designing the whole vehicle in CAD software, as well as doing different analysis, for example, Computer Fluid Dynamics (CFD), Buoyancy calculations, center of gravity and buoyancy, amongst others, to validate the design. The team is also in charge of manufacturing and assembling all the external structure, external components and all of Proteus watertight enclosures.
